federalLawText :: Sertraline (Zoloft) is unscheduled in the United States, but is available by prescription only. This means that sales and distribution are allowed only by those with a license and only to those with a prescription (according to FDA regulations). Federally, possession is not illegal even without a prescription. Some US States make it a crime to possess an unscheduled, prescription-only drug without a prescription.
