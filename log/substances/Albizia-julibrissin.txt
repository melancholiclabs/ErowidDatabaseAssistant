description :: Albizia julibrissin is a tree with fern-like leafed branches growing from 20 to 40 feet (6-12 m) tall. Produces pink globe-shaped feathery flowers in late spring and summer followed by 4 to 6 inch (10-15 cm) seed pods. A. julibrissin is native to Asia and is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat depression and anxiety.
