chemicalName :: N-(4-hydroxyphenyl)acetamide
description :: Acetaminophen is a non-salicylate analgesic and antipyretic (pain killer and fever reducer). It is a common over-the-counter pain medication found in hundreds of products around the world. At higher doses it is known to cause liver-damage and has a low therapeutic index (ratio of effective dose to toxic dose), making it dangerous when included in recreationally used pharmaceuticals. It is not known to be psychoactive.

Sales for human consumption are regulated by the FDA and each formulation or product requires specific FDA approval.
